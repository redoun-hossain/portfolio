RAG AI Knowledge Assistant

A production-ready Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) assistant built with n8n, OpenAI GPT-4o and Pinecone. It searches a company's own documents for the right answer, then replies through Telegram in seconds — so customers get accurate, source-grounded support around the clock.

Overview

What This Assistant Does

Instead of relying on an LLM's general knowledge, this assistant answers only from a company's own documents. Files stored in Google Drive are converted into embeddings and indexed in Pinecone, so every reply comes from real, verified content. When a customer messages Telegram with a question, n8n retrieves the closest matching passages and GPT-4o turns them into a clear, accurate answer. If the assistant isn't confident in a match, the conversation is escalated to a human agent instead of guessing.

Project Highlights

  • Built entirely in n8n, no custom backend required
  • OpenAI GPT-4o for grounded, accurate answers
  • Pinecone vector search for fast document retrieval
  • Automatic escalation to a human when confidence is low
  • Delivered through Telegram for instant, 24/7 replies
The Problem

Challenge & Solution

❗

The Challenge

The support team received hundreds of repetitive questions every day and spent hours digging through documentation to answer them. Customers often waited hours for a reply, especially outside office hours, which increased workload and hurt satisfaction.

✅

The Solution

A RAG assistant now indexes company documents automatically and answers questions in real time through Telegram. GPT-4o generates responses strictly from retrieved company knowledge, and low-confidence queries are routed to a human agent instead of being guessed at.
