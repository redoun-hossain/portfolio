📝 Project Overview

A RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) based AI assistant built entirely in n8n. It reads company documents from Google Drive, stores them as vector embeddings in Pinecone, and answers customer questions instantly via Telegram — with accurate, source-based answers instead of AI hallucinations. Complex queries are automatically escalated to a human agent.

❗ The Problem

The support team spent hours daily answering repetitive questions already covered in company documentation. Customers waited 4+ hours for replies, and after business hours there was no support at all — leading to lost sales and frustrated customers.

✅ The Solution

Built an n8n RAG pipeline: documents from Google Drive are automatically chunked, embedded via OpenAI, and stored in Pinecone. When a customer messages the Telegram bot, the workflow retrieves the most relevant document chunks and GPT-4 generates an accurate, source-grounded answer within seconds. Low-confidence answers trigger automatic human escalation with full conversation context.

📈 Business Impact

  • ✅ 80% of support tickets resolved automatically
  • ✅ Response time: 4 hours → under 10 seconds
  • ✅ 24/7 support without extra staff cost
  • ✅ Support team workload reduced by 50%
