— Project Overview —

What This Project Does

This project demonstrates a complete AI-powered Lead Generation Agent developed entirely with n8n. The agent automatically captures leads from Facebook Ads, website forms, and landing pages, uses OpenAI GPT-4o to analyze and qualify each lead based on custom criteria, and instantly distributes them to your sales team through HubSpot CRM and Slack notifications.

The workflow includes automatic lead validation, AI-powered qualification scoring, duplicate detection, personalized email responses, and comprehensive logging — ensuring your sales team never misses a hot lead. Every lead is captured, scored, and routed to the right person in real-time, turning cold traffic into qualified opportunities 24/7.

— The Story —

Challenge & Solution

⚠️

The Challenge

The business was losing hundreds of potential leads every month. Facebook Ads leads sat in the system for hours before someone contacted them, website form submissions were often missed, and there was no systematic way to identify high-quality prospects. Sales reps wasted time on unqualified leads while hot opportunities went cold — resulting in lost revenue and frustrated marketing spend.

✅

The Solution

A complete LeadGen AI Agent was built with n8n. It instantly captures leads from all sources, uses GPT-4o to analyze and score each lead based on custom criteria (budget, intent, industry, urgency), automatically syncs qualified leads to HubSpot, and sends instant Slack alerts to the right sales rep. Cold leads receive automated nurture sequences, while hot leads get immediate human attention — maximizing conversion at every stage.
