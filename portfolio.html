Lead Capture & CRM Automation

Challenge

The client was manually checking Facebook Ads Manager every few hours and copying leads into a spreadsheet. Leads were being missed and follow-up was delayed by hours.

Solution

Built an n8n workflow that triggers every 15 minutes, fetches new leads from the Facebook Lead Ads API, deduplicates them, adds to Airtable CRM, and sends a Slack notification plus a personalized welcome email automatically.

Tools Used

n8nFacebook Lead Ads APIAirtableGmailSlack

Result

✅ 100% of leads captured automatically
✅ Response time reduced from 4 hours to under 5 minutes
✅ Client saved 5+ hours per week
